Detailed Description

Constituent data structure.

The CFInfo data structure contains all information about one irreducible constituent of a module. Most of this information is useful only in the context of a Lat_Info structure.

dim is the dimension of the constituent, and num enumerates constituents of same dimension. mult ist the multiplicity of this constituent in the module. idword and idpol specify the identifying word for this module. The identifying word is used by the CHOP program.
